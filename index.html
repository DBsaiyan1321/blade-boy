BLADE BOY

Story: Oh no! You're broke because you spent too much money on that cool outfit. Originally, you were saving up for a cool blade to go along with your sword handle and sheath on your back, but that's going to have to wait because now you don't have enough money for rent. There is only one opportunity that you have to get money...

You must go to the mountains and risk your life to get gold coins so you can sell them for money. Only then will you be able to pay rent, and hopefully get a blade in the future. Use A W D or the arrow keys to move. Get those gold coins!
